Apple Cutting

APPLES! one of the easiest snacks you can eat at anytime. One of the most known quick snacks you can get, seen on every moive with a kid running out of the house chasing the bus down the street. Personally me my favorite one is the green, but sometimes I think the red is good but not the best.

Apple cutting steps

  1. Get a apple (which ever you choose)
  2. Put the apple of your choice down on a cutting bored
  3. Grab a knife and put it through the apple and place the knife in the apple 3 times different angles

Then boom you have apple slices. You don't always have to cut it up but you can also eat it whole.
