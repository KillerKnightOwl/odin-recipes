Tacos

Tacos a very amazing thing that are easy to eat quickly you can eat it with any type of meat that you want. The most common is asada/steak.

  1. Gather and cook the meat
  2. Warm the taco shells or tortillas
  3. Prepare toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and limes
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling the shells with cooked protein and adding desired toppings

And boom you have a taco that you are able to eat.
